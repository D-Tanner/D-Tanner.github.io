Intro

Hi! My name is Dillon Tanner. I am a graduate of App Academy, an immersive software coding bootcamp. Through over 1000 hours of code and working with others, I have proven capable of diving into a completely new technology stack and rapidly building a product. With hard work and perseverance, I have built 4 fully functioning web applications in 6 months. I have learned valuable lessons of teamwork, time management, and best industry practices. I could not be more excited to be a part of a team that builds a simple and elegant product that can help people across the globe.

I am confident my software experience, interpersonal communication skills, and passion for service will allow me to immediately be effective as a member of a team. At App Academy, I learned how to solve problems with a number of different technologies, including Javascript, React, Node.js, Python, Flask, Express, and PostgreSQL. I understand the basics of web development, and more importantly I have confidence jumping into and learning any new framework. App Academy taught me to be a programmer, problem solver, and an effective team member. I have experience delegating tasks, meticulously crushing bugs, and meeting deadlines. I take great pride in the work that I do, always making sure to have working code by a target date set before me.

Reaching out and collaborating with others is what granted me the ability to learn exponentially both at App Academy and in other industries. I never hesitate to ask for help and am always willing to make sure every team member is on the same page. It is why I value a strong team - and being an effective team member - above everything else.

I like to emphasize simplicity and a strong user experience in my work. Feel free to check out some of my projects here.

Work

Bikechain

Repo

In Bikechain, users can plan rides with local riders or by skill level in a user-friendly map UI. Users can also check out their friends rides to make sure they are up to date with all of the activity. Primary technologies used are React, Flask, Node, PostgreSQL. Styled with pure vanilla CSS.

CodeNote

Repo

With CodeNote, users can autosave snippets of code to make sure those brilliant functions are not forgotten. Users can also share and make copies of other users' notes, guaranteeing cooperation and accelerated knowledge. Technolgies used are PostgreSQL, Express, React, and Node (PERN stack).

JumpStart

Repo

Cloned from Kickstarter with GoFundMe influences, users can tell their story. With a simple UI, users can easily search for and find projects. Donations can even be made anyonymous. Technologies used are React, Flask, Node, PostgreSQL.

DinoFlow

Repo

Inspired by StackOverflow, it is a site built by dinosaurs, for dinosaurs. Creatures can ask concerning dinosaur-related questions, while other beasts that roam Pangea can answer and vote on the quality of the question. Technologies used are Javascript, Express, PostgreSQL, Sequelize, and PUG .
